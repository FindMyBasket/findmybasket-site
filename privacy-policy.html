Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

FindMyBasket ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Who we are

FindMyBasket is a UK skincare price comparison service operated by Robbie Coffin. Our website is findmybasket.co.uk. You can contact us at [email protected].

2. What information we collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address when you join our waitlist
  • Usage data such as pages visited, time on site and referring pages, collected via Google Analytics
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type and IP address, collected automatically

We do not collect payment information, sensitive personal data, or require you to create an account to use the service.

3. How we use your information

  • To send you updates about the FindMyBasket launch (if you joined the waitlist)
  • To improve the website and user experience using anonymised analytics data
  • To respond to any enquiries you send us

4. Affiliate links

FindMyBasket uses affiliate links to retailers. When you click through to a retailer and make a purchase, we may earn a commission. This is at no extra cost to you. We do not share your personal data with retailers as part of this process.

5. Email marketing

If you joined our waitlist, you consented to receive email updates about FindMyBasket. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send. We use Mailchimp to manage our email list.

6. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This uses cookies to collect anonymised data. No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

7. Data retention

We retain your email address on our waitlist until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 26 months in line with Google Analytics default settings.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

9. Third party services

We use the following third party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics - analytics
  • Mailchimp - email marketing
  • Supabase - database hosting
  • Vercel - website hosting
  • AWIN - affiliate network

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify waitlist subscribers of any significant changes by email. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at [email protected].
