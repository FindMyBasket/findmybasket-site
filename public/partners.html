Who we are

About FindMyBasket

FindMyBasket is a UK skincare price comparison platform that helps consumers find the best value way to buy their whole routine across major UK beauty retailers. Unlike single-product comparison tools, we optimise a full routine — looking at a user's complete basket, factoring in delivery thresholds, and recommending the optimal combination of retailers.

Our Product Finder covers 59 active ingredients and 47 skin concerns, helping users discover the right products for their skin type and find the best available price across UK retailers — all in one place.

We are pre-launch and actively building our audience across TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest and organic search. Our waitlist is growing and we are approaching full public launch.

How we work with brands

Partnership model

FindMyBasket works exclusively on a CPA affiliate model — we earn a small commission when users click through to a retailer and make a purchase. We do not charge brands for listing or placement, we do not run sponsored posts, and we do not accept payment to influence our recommendations.

This means every brand featured on FindMyBasket is there because of their product range and retail presence — and every price shown is real, verified and editorially independent.

Editorial standards

Our commitment to accuracy

  • We only feature products with verified, real prices sourced directly from retailer product feeds or manually confirmed
  • We never fabricate prices, inflate savings or show placeholder data
  • Affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed to users on all pages
  • Brand partnerships never influence our price comparison results — rankings are determined solely by price
  • We comply with UK ASA guidelines on affiliate marketing disclosure
