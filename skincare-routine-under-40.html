What a basic routine actually needs

Before spending anything, understand what a basic routine requires. For most people, the essentials are a cleanser, a moisturiser and an SPF. Everything else — serums, toners, eye creams, exfoliants — is optional. Get the basics right first.

The three-step routine of cleanse, moisturise, protect is all most people need and is what dermatologists consistently recommend as the foundation of good skin health.
