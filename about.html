Our story

Why we built FindMyBasket

It started with a simple frustration. The same skincare products — CeraVe, The Ordinary, La Roche-Posay — were available at completely different prices across different UK retailers. A four-product morning routine could cost £8-15 more depending on where you bought it. And yet no tool existed to compare prices across a whole routine at once.

Most price comparison tools work product by product. But that is not how people buy skincare. They buy a routine. So we built a tool that thinks the same way — one that looks at your whole basket, factors in delivery costs, and finds the optimal combination of retailers for your specific set of products.

FindMyBasket is also home to a Product Finder — a search tool covering 59 active ingredients and 47 skin concerns — that helps users discover the right products for their skin type and find the best price across UK retailers in one place.

Our values

How we work

🔍
Editorially independent
We are not paid to promote any brand or retailer. Our recommendations are driven entirely by price data. We earn a small affiliate commission when users shop through our links — at no extra cost to them.
✅
Accurate and honest
We only show products we actually track with real, verified prices. We never fabricate prices or show placeholder data. If we don't have a price, we say so and point users to Amazon instead.
🌱
Ingredient-educated audience
Our audience actively researches skincare ingredients and skin concerns before buying. They are informed, considered shoppers — not bargain hunters. They choose quality products at the best available price.
🇬🇧
Built for UK shoppers
UK prices, UK retailers, UK delivery thresholds. Every feature is designed specifically for the UK skincare market. No irrelevant international pricing or out-of-stock imports.
